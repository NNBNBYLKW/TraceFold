# Step 9 Chapter 6 Phase 1 Locked Boundaries

## Project Center

- TraceFold remains a local-first personal data workbench.
- It is not a single-domain app and not a universal platform container.

## Mainline

- The mainline remains `Capture -> Parse -> Pending -> Confirm -> Formal Record -> Query / Analysis / Derivation`.
- No special side path may bypass the confirm boundary into formal facts.

## Service Center

- The application service layer remains the only real business logic center.
- Routers remain thin.
- Repositories remain limited to query/add/flush/update responsibilities.

## Formal Fact Boundary

- Formal facts remain the most stable layer.
- Pending remains an intermediate review layer.
- Rule alerts remain derived reminders.
- AI derivations remain derived explanations.

## AI Boundary

- AI does not confirm, discard, force-insert, or mutate formal facts.
- AI language must not be used as if it were the formal system conclusion.

## Entry Boundaries

- Web remains the main business interface.
- Desktop remains shell-only.
- Telegram remains a lightweight adapter.
- No entry may become a second business center.

## Workbench Role Boundary

- Template = named work mode
- Shortcut = high-frequency fixed entry
- Recent = continue-work entry
- Dashboard = summary layer

These roles are locked for Phase 1 and must not be merged casually.

## Non-Goal Boundary

- automation engine remains out of scope
- graph-centered recentering remains out of scope
- health-to-medical-tool drift remains out of scope
- desktop-heavy business client remains out of scope
- Telegram management-client drift remains out of scope
- multi-tenant/distributed/platform engineering expansion remains out of scope
